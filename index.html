A neighborhood bar created for locals and visitors alike.

Enjoy casual drinks in a relaxed environment.

As lights dim and cocktails are poured, an exciting, eclectic mix of vinyls is curated by LA's finest DJs. Socialize, dance, or lounge while being surrounded by floor to ceiling records and grooves that set the mood for a convivial night fueled by cocktails and music.

DJs Every Single Night

Wall to wall records and grooves!

Gold Line Bar is located in Los Angeles, CA. There are 10,000+ records from Peanut Butter Wolf's personal collection in the bar's library. DJs play nightly and select records exclusively from the collection.
