A neighborhood bar created for locals and visitors alike.

Enjoy casual drinks in a relaxed environment.

As lights dim and cocktails are poured, a warm, social dining experience begins with plates drawn from diverse cultures and local twists. In the later hours, programming infuses energy to ensure the mood is right for a convivial night fueled by cocktails, music and late-night bites.

North Hollywoods Local Hangout

An awesome little neighborhood cocktail bar in North Hollywood.
This hidden gem, nestled in the Magnolia Square is packed with incredible cocktails, great vibes and a killer soundtrack. We aim to do some good within our community by trying our best to have a positive influence on our guests.
